Insights

This double pie chart, filters out students based on GPA, revealing the distributions of stress levels from each GPA range, from this we can see that Students with a GPA lower than 2.5 mostly have low stress levels, while students with a GPA higher than 3.5 have a majority high stress level, with 1 outlier having low stress levels.
