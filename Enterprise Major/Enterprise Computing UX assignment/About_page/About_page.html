    Our story

    The Macfields gym was created 20 years ago when the gyms founder, Jared Jones was looking for gym nearby but was unable to find one. Jones was someone who really cared about his health and he wanted his community to have a gym nearby, unable to find any gyms Jones decided to found his own.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    What age do I have to be to join?

    You are allowed to join at the age of 16

    Do I need to bring my own towel?

    Yes, you will need to bring your own towel, so that you can dry off and keep the equipment sweat-free for the next user.

    What time is the gym open?

    The macfields gym is open everyday from 7am-9pm.

    How can I contact the gym?

    You can contact the gym by calling us at 0454 331 982 or by emailing us at Macfields_gym@gmail.com.

    How can I cancel the membership?

    Your membership can be cancelled by asking the receptionist.

    Do group training session cost anything

    No, group training sessions are free and held every Friday.
