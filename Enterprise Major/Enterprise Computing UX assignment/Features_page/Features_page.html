    Cafe

    The cafe is an important feature of the Macfields gym which is frequently used by many gym-goers. The cafe allows gym-goers to have convenient access to food and drinks that are perfect for pre-workout and post-workout meals. The cafe is located at the reception.

    Rental Hall

    Another important feature of the Macfields gym is the rental hall. The rental hall is a feature that is frequently used for community sports events or schools. The hall can be rented by members for $50 an hour and there is no limit to the amount of people who can be in the hall.
